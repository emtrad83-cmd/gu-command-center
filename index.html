⏰ Daily Reminder — Mon–Fri at 7:00 PM

Since this is a web app, push notifications require a bit of setup. Here are three easy options:

Option 1 — iPhone/Android Alarm: Set a recurring alarm labeled Daily Shipped Log at 7:00 PM, Mon–Fri.

Option 2 — Google Calendar: Create a recurring event "Fill Daily Shipped" at 7 PM, Mon–Fri, with a notification.

Option 3 — Add to Home Screen: In Safari or Chrome, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". Then use a shortcut/automation to open the app at 7 PM.

📅 Weekly Mentor Review — Sunday at 8:00 AM

Set a Sunday 8:00 AM reminder labeled Weekly GU Review. Open the app, go to the Reviews tab, and tap "Generate This Week's Mentor Review" to get your full Sunday debrief.

📈 Monthly Review

On the last day of each month, open the Reviews tab and tap "Generate This Month's Review" for your full trends, strengths, and growth opportunities.
